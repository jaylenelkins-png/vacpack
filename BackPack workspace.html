The Problem

Commercial cleaning teams are forced to choose between heavy backpack vacuums that cause fatigue, underpowered cordless units that require multiple passes, and loud machines that disrupt hotels, schools, and daytime operations.

The industry has accepted these tradeoffs for decades. Weight, power, and noise have never been solved together in one system.

Operational Pain Points

Heavy Equipment

Backpack vacuums strain operators, increasing fatigue and injury risk across long shifts.

Insufficient Power

Cordless units often lack suction, forcing more time per room and higher labor costs.

Excessive Noise

Loud motors limit use in hotels, schools, airports, and other noise‑sensitive environments.

The Vacpack Solution

Vacpack is a premium cordless commercial backpack vacuum engineered to be 20% lighter than leading models while delivering class‑leading suction and significantly quieter operation.

High‑RPM motor technology, carbon‑fiber and magnesium construction, and a pro‑grade harness system combine to create a new standard for commercial cleaning performance.

Engineering Features

High‑RPM Motor

Ultra‑high‑speed brushless motor delivers powerful suction with a smaller, lighter assembly. Precision balancing and high‑quality bearings support quiet, efficient operation.

Carbon‑Fiber & Magnesium

Carbon‑fiber shell provides lightweight strength, while a magnesium motor housing manages heat and vibration without adding unnecessary weight.

Quiet‑Performance Design

Multi‑layer acoustic insulation, tuned airflow paths, and Helmholtz‑style silencers reduce harsh motor tones and turbulence noise.
